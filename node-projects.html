Node Projects

Projects focused on cloud infrastructure and services hosting. While they may process blockchain, that is not their primary purpose.

ProjectCategoryDescriptionPrime Moover NotesWebsite
FluxComputingDecentralized SaaS, Compute, Storage. FLUX token. Requires ZelCore Wallet.Long time operator. First mover advantage, real tech. Excellent first project. Tikicow.com hosted on Flux.runonflux.io
TimpiPaaS / SearchDecentralized web indexing. NTIMPI token. Limited operator licenses.Significant investor and node operator. Early stages, unique market. Limiting nodes shows commitment to non-dilution.timpi.io
Render NetworkGPU ComputingDecentralized GPU rendering. RNDR token.Growing market but many competitors and high cost. Not for me.render.x.io
DeepLinkRemote/GamingCloud gaming service with low latency. DLP tokens.Unique project. Purchased NFT/Licenses for speculation. May not be English-first.deeplink.cloud
Akash NetworkComputingDecentralized cloud computing marketplace. AKT token.Good project, active community, clear monetization. Would consider if starting new.akash.network
DeEEP (NerdNodez)ComputingDecentralized node hosting. $DEEEP token.Very early. Purchased hardware nodes. Clear revenue model. Hardware reusable if project fails.deeep.network
FilecoinStorageDecentralized storage network. FIL token.Mature, focused, clear revenue. Lots of competition in commodity space. Not for me.filecoin.io
NeoxaGaming & CryptoBlockchain + gaming with PoW and PoG. 21B NEOX supply, 4-year halving.Moderate investor. Unique as overlay on existing titles vs total platform approach.neoxa.net
StorjStorageCloud storage using unused hard drives. STORJ token. Raised $30M.Another distributed storage, no unique angle. Not for me.storj.io
IPFSFile SystemPeer-to-peer hypermedia protocol. Open-source, no native token.Not a hosting project per se—the underlying protocol many storage projects use. Listed for awareness.ipfs.tech
MyriaGaming & BlockchainBlockchain gaming ecosystem, Ethereum L2. $MYRIA token. 14 investors.Investor. Diversity of A-rated titles and clear market strategy gives confidence.myria.com
ArweavePermanent StoragePermanent data storage. One-time fee. AR token. $22M from a16z.Specific target market and fee approach. Not for me.arweave.org
SiaStorageBlockchain cloud storage. Siacoin. $6.4M funding.Older project with first mover advantage. Mined this years ago. Top of list if considering storage.sia.tech
SwarmStorageDecentralized storage/comms on Ethereum. BZZ token.Unique: permanent webpage on mainstream blockchain. Not for me but interesting.ethswarm.org
Crust NetworkStorageIPFS-compatible decentralized storage. CRU token.Newer entrant to commodity space. Not for me.crust.network
io.netGPU ComputingDecentralized GPU cloud on Solana. 100K+ GPUs connected, 1M+ compute hours delivered. $IO token. Raised $40M Series A led by Hack VC and Multicoin Capital. Serves Stability AI, Filecoin and others.Strong competitor to Akash in the GPU cloud space. Solana integration means low-latency settlement. Impressive traction with real AI clients. Hourly rewards model is transparent. Worth comparing against Akash and Render for anyone considering GPU DePIN.io.net
AethirGPU Cloud / GamingDecentralized GPU cloud for AI inference and cloud gaming. $141M+ annualized recurring revenue. Enterprise-grade nodes. ATH token. Largest decentralized GPU cloud by revenue in Web3.Revenue numbers are eye-opening—$55M+ in a single month (Jan 2026). Focuses on enterprise clients and cloud gaming which gives it a different angle than Render. High barrier to entry for node operators (enterprise-grade hardware). If numbers are real, this is a top-tier DePIN project.aethir.com
BittensorDecentralized AI/MLDecentralized machine learning network. AI models collaborate in subnets and earn TAO tokens based on performance. Top DePIN by market cap. Strong institutional interest—Grayscale Bittensor Trust.The big brain of DePIN—decentralized AI model training and inference. Subnet architecture is clever; different subnets specialize in different AI tasks. Grayscale trust filing is a major institutional signal. Complex to participate in but represents the frontier of decentralized AI.bittensor.com
NosanaGPU InferenceDecentralized GPU compute on Solana focused on AI inference. Connects idle GPU resources to AI workloads. NOS token.More focused than io.net—specializes specifically in inference rather than general GPU compute. Solana-native. Smaller but differentiated. Good for those already in the Solana ecosystem.nosana.io
Golem NetworkComputingOne of the oldest decentralized compute platforms. Users rent idle computing power for AI, simulations, and rendering. GLM token. ~$278M market cap.OG of decentralized compute—predates most DePIN projects. Solid but hasn't had the breakout moment newer projects have seen. Established community and tech stack. Worth watching if they can capture AI demand wave.golem.network
