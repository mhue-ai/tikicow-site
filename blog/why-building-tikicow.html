Why I'm Building Tikicow.com: Unbiased, Structured Information in the DePIN Space

By Zach Miller (Prime Moover) · Nov 5, 2024

Tikicow.com isn't just a passion project; it's a place where we can explore, learn, and grow together in the rapidly evolving world of decentralized technology.

1. Supporting the Community with Unbiased Information

In an industry often driven by speculation and profit motives, objective, unbiased information is essential. My commitment is to present facts, weigh the pros and cons, and let you decide what aligns with your goals.

2. The Value of Structured Evaluations

By creating a standardized evaluation methodology, each project is presented in a clear, consistent, and comprehensive way. You can explore projects side-by-side and make decisions based on thorough analysis.

3. Building a Sustainable Business

Monetization strategies will align with unbiased information—affiliate partnerships with reputable projects, ad revenue, premium content, or community support—while ensuring transparency and integrity.

What Tikicow.com Means for You

  • Confidence in your decisions through structured evaluations.
  • A sense of community to learn, share, and grow together.
  • Access to sustainable insights with transparency at the forefront.
