How to Evaluate a DePIN Project: A Comprehensive Guide

By Zach Miller (Prime Moover) · Nov 5, 2024

With so many emerging projects, how can you tell which ones are worth your time? This guide walks through a step-by-step methodology for assessing DePIN projects.

1. Problem-Solution Fit

Does the project solve a real problem? How effective is the solution? What's the value proposition?

2. Competitor Analysis and Differentiation

Identify competitors and evaluate unique advantages in approach, technology, or incentives.

3. Revenue Model and Scalability

Where do revenue streams come from? Is reporting transparent? How is revenue shared with stakeholders?

4. Hardware and Node Network Feasibility

Are hardware requirements accessible? Is the network activity consistent and scalable?

5. Team, Funding, and Endorsements

Team experience, funding status, investor support, and industry endorsements.

6. Tokenomics and Governance

Examine token distribution, vesting schedules, emission schedules, and governance models.

7. Roadmap and Milestones

Look for clarity, feasibility, and a history of meeting milestones.

8. Transparency and References

Evaluate operational transparency, verifiable data access, and community engagement.
